Visesh Chari

Sunnyvale, California

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at the Computer Vision, Machine Learning (CVML) group at Amazon
Lab126, Sunnyvale, USA. Prior to joining Amazon, I was a Visiting Researcher at
https://cvit.iiit.ac.in, and before that I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Willow group at
https://www.di.ens.fr/willow.

I spent four beautiful years doing my PhD right below the French Alpes, at INRIA Rhône Alpes (https://team.inria.fr/perception/).

Over the last few years, I have had the opportunity to work with amazing people like Peter Sturm (https://team.inria.fr/steep/people/peter-sturm/), Ivan Laptev (https://www.di.ens.fr/~laptev/), Josef Sivic (https://www.di.ens.fr/~josef/), CV Jawahar (https://faculty.iiit.ac.in/~jawahar/), PJ Narayanan (https://faculty.iiit.ac.in/~pjn/) and several others.

While not working, or discussing AI with friends, I spend my time hiking, biking, running along west cliff drive (https://www.santacruz.org/listings/west-cliff-drive/) and occasionally dabbling in sports like cricket, ping pong and tennis.
